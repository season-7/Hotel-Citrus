Our Wedding Grounds

Hotel Citrus provides an elegant and unique setting for wedding celebrations as well as beach weddings in Sri Lanka combined with impeccable service and a delectable banquet of award-winning cuisine.

Whether a beach wedding, garden wedding or a grand wedding in our elegantly designed ballrooms, or even if you want a combination of all three, there is nothing more that you desire than a perfect wedding day. But for us, everything is more than just perfect; it's spectacular. It's not just intimate, it's extravagant. Our dedicated team of professionals will give you a comprehensive wedding service while simplifying your wedding planning, ensuring your wedding day will be unforgettable and uniquely yours.

Choose from our specially designed menus or simply tell us what you would like us to serve your guests.

Our packages include options for themed bridal showers and bachelor parties as well.
