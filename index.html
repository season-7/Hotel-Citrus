About Us

Hotel Citrus is the oldest hotel in the city, having been established in 1902 by English businessman Harrison Kamau, when the city was a railway halt.
We offer a wide range of services.

Swimming Pool

Hotel Citrus has a 20 meter heated swimming pool, suitable for swimmers of all standards with a depth of 1.3meters, plus for that starting out a children’s pool. There are pool programmes and activities including aqua aerobics, adults and kids swimming lessons available weekly. We also offer private one to one and group swimming lessons.

Catering

Complete Catering Service offers you a friendly, flexible approach for all your catering needs.
Whether you require a simple sandwich buffet delivered to your office, a party at home or a fully served banquet.

Accomodation

We offer accommodation based on the number of beds available.

Conference facilities

The main conference facilities include 2 large conference rooms with a maximum capacity of 100 delegates in each and one medium conference room for up to 30 theatre.

Fitness Classes

Sixty minute group classes – including yoga, Pilates and spinning – are offered throughout the week. Participants who are not staying at the Resort may enjoy the lesson for CAD 30, which includes access to our fitness and changing room facilities.
