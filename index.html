About Us

Hotel Citrus is the oldest hotel in the city, having been established in 1902 by English businessman Harrison Kamau, when the city was a railway halt.
We offer a wide range of services:

  • Accomodation
  • Conference facilities
  • Spa
  • Catering

Services Offered

Hotel citrus has 217 rooms, including 160 deluxe rooms, 32 club rooms, two courier singles, 21 themed suites, a presidential suite, and a penthouse. Suites have a living room and guest water closet, as well as the bedroom (two bedrooms in some suites). The presidential suite also has a bodyguard room. There are nine meeting and conference rooms with audio-visual equipment, including a 227-square-metre (2,440 sq ft) ballroom, and a business center with computers, fax, photocopier, and secretarial services. Hotel Citrus has a heated rooftop swimming pool, as well as a health club with a sauna and steambath. There is a shopping arcade located on the first floor with gift shops, a boutique, a chemist, and an optician.

Awards

In 2015, hotel citrus was number 10 in th list of top 25 hotels in the world. We also won travellers choice award during the same year and we have been nominated for several other awards this year.
